TCF Bleaching & 91% Eco-Friendly Paper

Total Chlorine-Free (TCF) Bleaching

At PlanetPulp, our commitment to sustainability is embodied in our Total Chlorine-Free (TCF) bleaching process. We use hydrogen peroxide, a safe and fully biodegradable agent, instead of chlorine-based chemicals. This innovation ensures zero chlorine emissions into air or water, significantly reducing environmental harm and protecting aquatic life.

91% Eco-Friendly Paper Production

Through strategic processes and innovation, PlanetPulp produces paper that’s 91% eco-friendly. Here’s how:

  • 100% recycled fibers—drastically reducing deforestation.
  • Closed-loop water systems—saving water and preventing waste discharge.
  • Non-toxic coatings—enhancing quality while staying biodegradable.

Our ultimate goal is achieving 100% sustainability, without compromising on quality, usability, or aesthetics.
