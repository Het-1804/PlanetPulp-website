Why Choose PlanetPulp?

Gujarat's first 91% eco-friendly Paper Production and Solutions brand.

At PlanetPulp, we are redefining sustainability through innovation in paper production.Our mission is to create an eco-conscious alternative to conventional paper by utilizing recycled materials and ensuring a zero-waste manufacturing process. Unlike traditional methods that rely on harsh chemicals, we employ hydrogen peroxide as a bleaching agent, a significantly cleaner and more environmentally responsible choice.

Every byproduct generated in our process is repurposed—wastewater and organic residues are transformed into nutrient-rich vermicompost, reinforcing our commitment to a circular economy.

We don’t just produce paper; we craft a future where sustainability and efficiency go hand in hand. At PlanetPulp, we envision a world where paper production nurtures the planet rather than depletes it—where every sheet tells a story of responsibility, resilience, and renewal.
